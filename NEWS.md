# forkflow 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* `spec_grid()` and `adjuster_sets()` enumerate a multiverse of defensible
  specifications.
* `fit_multiverse()` fits a model across the whole grid, optionally in parallel
  via furrr.
* `voe()` summarises the vibration of effects (relative ratio, Janus fraction).
* `spec_curve()` and `voe_volcano()` visualise the distribution of estimates.
* `report_multiverse()` writes a reproducible summary of the multiverse.
